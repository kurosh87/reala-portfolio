## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy outlines the rules and guidelines for using our services. It is designed to protect our users, our infrastructure, and the broader internet community. All users must comply with this policy when using our platform.

## 2. Prohibited Activities

You may not use our services to:

### 2.1 Illegal Activities
- Engage in any activity that violates applicable local, state, national, or international law
- Facilitate or promote illegal transactions
- Distribute content that is unlawful or promotes unlawful activities

### 2.2 Harmful Content
- Distribute malware, viruses, or other malicious code
- Engage in phishing, spoofing, or other deceptive practices
- Send unsolicited bulk messages (spam)
- Host or distribute content that exploits minors

### 2.3 Abuse of Services
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to any system, network, or account
- Interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of our services
- Use our services to conduct denial-of-service attacks
- Circumvent any access controls or usage limits

### 2.4 Intellectual Property Violations
- Infringe upon the intellectual property rights of others
- Distribute pirated software, media, or other copyrighted material
- Use our services to facilitate trademark or patent infringement

## 3. Resource Usage

Users must use system resources responsibly. Excessive consumption of CPU, memory, bandwidth, or storage that impacts other users or system stability may result in throttling or suspension.

## 4. Security

Users are responsible for:

- Maintaining the security of their accounts and credentials
- Promptly reporting any security vulnerabilities or incidents
- Implementing appropriate security measures for their applications
- Keeping software and dependencies up to date

## 5. Monitoring and Enforcement

We reserve the right to monitor usage for compliance with this policy. Violations may result in:

- Warning notification
- Temporary suspension of services
- Permanent termination of account
- Legal action where appropriate

## 6. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this policy, please report it to abuse@company.com. We will investigate all reports and take appropriate action.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated through our platform or via email.
